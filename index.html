Our Strong Team

The Team behind PathGeo: A Team of Extensive Experience and a Passion to Make Your Lives Easier

Dr. Ming-Hsiang Tsou (CEO)

Dr. Ming-Hsiang Tsou is the Co-Founder and CEO of PathGeo Inc. As a Professor of Geography at San Diego State University, Ming is an international expert in Web GIS, Internet Mapping, Mobile GIS, Big Data, and Social Media. He is co-author of the book, Internet GIS and served on the editorial boards of the Annals of GIS (2008- 2013) and the Professional Geographers (2011- Present). In 2010, Tsou served as the Principle Investigator (PI) of a NSF-CDI project,” Mapping ideas from Cyberspace to Realspace”, $1.3 million funded by National Science Foundation, CDI-Type II Award # 1028177 (2010-2014). http://mappingideas.sdsu.edu/.Tsou also received the 2004 and 2010 Outstanding Faculty Award, and 2012 The President’s Leadership Fund (PLF) Faculty and Staff Awards for Excellence.

Dr. Chin-Te Jung
(CTO)

Dr. Chin-Te (Calvin) Jung is the chief GIS developer of PathGeo Inc. He received Ph.D. degree in geography from National Taiwan University in 2012. Calvin mainly takes charge of: designing main system framework and flowchart of PathGeo’s products leading and cooperating developers using API and GIS Web services with jQuery, python, and MongoDB to implement the products.

Chris Allen

Chris is a Ph.D. student in geography at San Diego State, and his interests include social media, web mapping, and historical GIS. He has a background in computer science and worked as a software engineer for several years in the aerospace industry. As part of the alpha team at PathGeo, Chris’s strengths are server-side programming, database management and data collection.

Dr. Chih-Yuan Chen

Dr. Chih-Yuan Chen finished his Ph.D. program at National Taiwan University in 2011. Before he joined the PathGeo, he was a research fellow in the National University of Singapore(NUS). His research interests fall under the knowledge discovery framework for geographical database with focus on geo-visualization and neural network. He is also interested in theoretical theme of cyberinfrastructure and geographic information science. Current topics of interest include spatial ontology, spatial provenance, semantic web, and social network analysis.

Jim Bates
(Senior Political Advisor)

Former Congressman Jim Bates joins PathGeo with a wealth of political experience. As the Senior Political Advisor for PathGeo he has served in elective office as a United States Congressman, San Diego County Supervisor, and San Diego City Councilman. He understands the requirements necessary for winning campaigns and elections. Born in Denver, Colorado in 1941, he started working on a farm in Mankato, Kansas at 14, then serving with distinction in the United States Marine Corps. He attended college at night, while working during the day as a Loan Officer for Bank of America. Jim graduated from San Diego State University. Jim Bates is well prepared to advise PathGeo.

John Hong
(Senior Technical Advisor)

John Hong is a Senior Technical Advisor at PathGeo, focused on the ElectionPath Application, and is also a Systems Engineer for the U.S. Department of the Navy. His interest include social media, current affairs, data analytics, cloud computing, and computer programming. John holds a Bachelors degree in Electrical Engineering from Marquette University and formerly a researcher for the University of Chicago / Argonne National Laboratory.

Su Yeon Han
