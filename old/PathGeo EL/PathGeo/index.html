Social Media Mapping

In 2012, over 340 million Tweets and 2.7 billion Facebook comments were generated each day. Most social media messages have been created by smart phones collecting GPS coordinates or user-defined location information. This dynamic supply of big data makes it difficult to quickly gain business insight from millions of social media messages without an intelligent analytical framework.

To address this challenge, PathGeo provides effective geo-targeting, real-time, and impact detection tools based on our unique patent-pending social media analytical framework. By interpreting the interconnected information components (TIME, PLACE, and MESSAGES), our products can help business to identify and reach new customers, improve marketing strategies, assess market campaign impacts, and monitor online reputations with actionable business intelligence.
