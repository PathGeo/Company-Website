MapTime Features

From Spreadsheet to Map

MapTime provides fast and reliable geocoding service to map your data in various format, such as xls or csv.

Multiple Visulization

MapTime provides multiple visualizations, such as marker map, cluster map, and hotspot map, for you to choose and visulize your data.

Download your data with coordinates

Just one click to download your data with latitude and longitude coordinates.
