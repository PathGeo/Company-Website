CityBuddy Features

CityBuddy uses crowd-sourcing and spatial-intelligence technology to collect and analyze social media (Twitter, YouTube, and Facebook) and web contents about the hot events nearby users’ locations with our patent-pending ranking algorithm for hot events. CityBuddy can provide actionable consumer intelligence with the following features

Dynamic Updating Event Information

with pictures and comments from social media.

Location-based Search

using users’ current GPS location or selecting a city or a place

Popular Ranking

Real-time ranking events from social media

Where are HOT events near me?

When people search multiple web sites and multiple social media channels to get relevant event information (conferences, sports games, music concerts), they need to know which events are popular and nearby their locations. User need to get the most updated event information with reviews, comments, pictures, and videos to help them make decisions about going to the event or know more about the event.

Browse Hot events

People can browse hot events in a week, nearby their locations, or search by keywords.

Search Result

CityBuddy ranks each events and collect relevant photos, videos, and comments from social media, including Facebook and Twitter

Hot Spot Map

People can switch to Map view to see the hot spots where people are discussing right now about events.
