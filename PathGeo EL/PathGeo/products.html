Map Your Customers

MapTime is a web-based mapping tool that allows users to geo-analyze their customer databases and to link them with census and demographic data in different zip codes, regions, and neighborhoods.

SocialTime is a real-time web-based social media geo-search tool that allows users to type keywords to retrieve social media feedbacks, online reputations, and customer opinions from a geo-targeted region (user-defined).

PathGeo Geo-Targeting APIs are a series of social media search engines (for Twitter, Flickr, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook) with geo-targeting capabilities which can be used for advance social media analytics and marketing analysis.

PathGeo Social Media Analytics Reports are comprehensive consulting services for customizable social media search result analysis, multiple keyword analysis, reputation management, event detections, and marketing campaign analysis.
