PathGeo

PathGeo is a spinoff of a $1.3 million San Diego State University project funded by the National Science Foundation. PathGeo's team members include marketing experts, Web GIS programmers, spatial statisticians, cartographers, social media API programmers, and natural language processing developers. The team is led by Dr. Ming-Hsiang Tsou, a renowned Internet GIS expert and SDSU Geography Professor.
